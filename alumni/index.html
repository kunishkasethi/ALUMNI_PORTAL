Manpreet Sharma, NITJ Alumnus achieves All India Rank 83 in UPSC

Manpreet Sharma, an alumnus of NIT Jalandhar’s 2016 Mechanical Engineering batch, has demonstrated Exceptional Academic and Professional Achievements. Hailing from the village of Lopon in the Moga District of Punjab, he comes from a family deeply dedicated to Public Service. His Mother is an Economics Lecturer at the Government Higher Secondary School in Moga, and his Father has dedicated nearly three decades to the field of Technical Education. His sister, Dr. Suman Sharma, is currently pursuing Postdoctoral Research in Horticulture at the University of Florence, USA, after completing her PhD at New Mexico State University, USA.
 

During his time at NIT Jalandhar, Manpreet ranked as the 4th topper of his batch. He displayed remarkable passion in his final-year project on the topic “Design and Fabrication of a Solar Thermal Storage Device using Phase Change Material,” under the guidance of Dr. Subash Chander. He also gained valuable Industry exposure through his summer internship at Tata Steel, Jamshedpur.


Manpreet began his professional career as an Assistant Manager at Maruti Suzuki India, where he served for three years. Driven by his aspiration to contribute to public administration, he later resigned to prepare for the Civil Services Examination. During this period, he actively engaged in social work through initiatives like ‘Mera Pind 360’ and founded ‘Mera Farmhouse,’ an agritech startup aimed at promoting rural development.


His perseverance paid off when he successfully cleared the UPSC Civil Services Examination, achieving an All India Rank of 83 in the reserve list of 2023, which was released on 25th October 2024.


Prof. Binod Kumar Kanaujia, Director of NIT Jalandhar, extends his Heartfelt Congratulations to Manpreet Sharma for his Outstanding Achievements and conveys his best wishes for Manpreet's Future Endeavors. His success is a source of great pride for the Institute, and we look forward to seeing his continued excellence in his journey of Service and Leadership.

An MoU signed between NIT Jalandhar and KISCO India Private Limited (KIPL)

The MoU was signed by Prof. Binod Kumar Kanaujia, Director of NIT Jalandhar, and Mr. Rohit Khanna, Managing Director of KIPL. The main objective of this collaboration is to extend vital financial support to academically gifted yet economically challenged students at NIT Jalandhar.
